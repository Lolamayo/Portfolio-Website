Twenty Five Archive



Twenty Five Archive is a project and label of the same name built on a passion for self-expression, experimentation, textile upcycling, and recycling.
All one-off garments are made slowly and sustainably by Lola Mayo in Naarm, 'Australia'.
Garments are available to purchase through email, Instagram, or can be made to order. Enquire within.
